Welcome

 

Greetings! I’m a software engineer working on end-to-end video recommendation solutions at Facebook. I am one of the first few ranking engineers within the team and having witnessed the product growing from the first Reels launch to nowadays the Unified Video Player. I have led various 0-to-1 initiatives, for example:

  • Reels early-stage ranking as a lightweight ranking layer that bridges retrieval and ranking that have contributed to double-digits Reels watch time increase since its inception.
  • Contextual video chaining that delivers a real-time responsive chaining experience with recommendations highly customized based on the entry point surface and the seed video (demo).

Prior to Meta, I led the modeling team within Walmart’s Customer org to build customer-centric data science and machine learning solutions to growing Walmart’s customer lifetime value and the share of wallet. I was also one of the founding data scientists for Walmart’s membership program, Walmart+, and built ML models on Walmart+ customer acquisition, conversion, retention, reactivation, and lifetime value for informed marketing decisions. Earlier on, I worked as quantitative researchers at financial institutions such as Deutsche Bank, Goldman Sachs, J.P. Morgan.

I received PhD in Statistics from Department of Statistics at Rutgers University, New Brunswick, advised by Professor Minge Xie and Professor Regina Y. Liu, and BSc in Statistics from University of Science and Technology of China.
